Questions

Asked all the time

Is this safe?

Every patient is assessed by a UK-registered doctor before any treatment is considered, and monitored weekly throughout. Medication is only prescribed where clinically appropriate.

How much does it cost?

Plans start from £129 a month with everything included — consultations, check-ins and clinical support. No hidden fees, and you can cancel anytime. (Illustrative pricing for this concept.)

What if medication isn't right for me?

Then we'll tell you honestly. The doctor builds a plan around what's clinically right for you — medication is one tool, not the whole answer.

How quickly can I start?

The check takes two minutes, a doctor typically reviews your case within one working day, and treatment can begin the same week where appropriate.
